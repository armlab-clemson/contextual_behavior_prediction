Scenario for an passive lane change. 
The vehicle comes across a slower vehicle in the lane, as a result, wants to switch lane. The driver maintains the speed and changes lane gradually. The driver is classified as passive.
1. There is no vehicle in the adjoining lane in this case.
2. No measurement or process noise.
3. front vehicle(slower) is slower by 3.33 mps velocity, starts with a front distance of 80m (instead of 100m in the other scenarios). the driver thresholds are 60m for both the drivers.
