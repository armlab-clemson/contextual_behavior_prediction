Scenario for an aggressive lane change. 
The vehicle comes across a slower vehicle in the lane, as a result, wants to switch lane. The driver starts accelerating and then makes a quick lane change. The driver is classified as aggressive. 
1. There is no vehicle in the adjoining lane in this case.
2. No measurement or process noise.
3. front vehicle(slower) is slower by 3.33 mps velocity. the driver thresholds are 60m for both the drivers.
